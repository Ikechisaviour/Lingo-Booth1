# Lingo Booth For Universities

**Structured language practice across web and mobile.**

Lingo Booth helps universities extend language learning beyond class time with guided lessons, conversation practice, flashcards, quizzes, writing practice, progress tracking, certificates, and institution subscriptions. The platform supports 20 languages as both learning targets and learner interface/native languages, making it suitable for language departments, study abroad programs, international student support, and continuing education.

## Why Universities Care

- Gives students structured practice between live class sessions
- Supports speaking, writing, review, and confidence-building in one platform
- Works across web and mobile so learners can practice wherever they are
- Supports multiple native-to-target language pairs rather than a single-language program
- Gives programs a path to cohort-based access, reporting, and expansion

## Recommended First Pilot

**6-week founding institution pilot**  
**Cohort:** up to 100 learners  
**Scope:** one department, course, study abroad cohort, or student support program  
**Included:** learner access, instructor/admin access where available, launch support, midpoint check-in, end-of-pilot summary  
**Draft fee:** $2,500, credited toward an annual subscription if the university continues within 30 days

## Success Metrics

- 75%+ invited learner activation
- 60%+ activated learners completing two or more weekly practice sessions
- Learners use at least three practice modes
- Faculty or staff can identify engagement patterns from available reporting
- Clear decision on renewal, expansion, or next cohort

## Best-Fit University Use Cases

- Language departments that want more out-of-class practice
- Study abroad offices preparing students before travel
- International student support programs
- Continuing education or alumni language programs
